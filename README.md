# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This is an end-to-end Customer Churn Analysis project built using **SQL, Excel, and Power BI**. The objective is to identify the key factors that contribute to customer churn, measure its financial impact, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecom companies because losing customers directly impacts revenue and profitability. This project analyzes customer behavior to identify high-risk customer segments, understand churn patterns, and support data-driven retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns.
* Identify high-risk customer segments.
* Measure churn rate and revenue at risk.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## 💡 Key Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate.
* **Fiber optic** customers churn more frequently than DSL customers.
* Customers paying higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.
* Most churn occurs during the first **12 months**.
* Customers without **tech support** or **online security** have significantly higher churn.
* **Electronic check** is the most common payment method among churned customers.
* A significant portion of revenue is at risk due to customer churn.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to switch to longer-term contracts.
* Bundle tech support and online security services.
* Promote automatic payment methods.
* Target customers during their first year with retention campaigns.
* Provide loyalty offers for high-value customers with high monthly charges.
